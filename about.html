Our Story

Epochal Technologies was formed whimsically to create an ethically derived and anonymized database of the US population with a property UX/UI and querying front end to enable users to locate populations of interest for marketing, capital budgeting and operations planning.
Epochal has an MVP of the product!In 2021 Epochal realized that its partnership with overseas sources of technical talent had enormous potential for US companies suffering from technical staff shortages, and desorous of aking the capital available to them to go a long way. Michael Parkan joined Epochal with a history of having created technical companies especially one that had over a hundred off shore resources and he had successfully catered to the technical needs of several clients in the US. Michael heads up Epochal now and is building technical staff provision services such as:

  • Staff Augmentation
  • Fractional CTO's
  • Technical Project Development
  • Staff Augmentation
